Side A · The Story

Vinyl on the walls,
halibut in the basket.

Hooky’s opened in a corner of Wellington Market on Front St West with a fryer, a chalkboard, and a stack of records borrowed from KOPS Records. The records stayed. The chalkboard got bigger. The line got longer.

The mural behind the counter is hand-painted — sandcastles, schools of fish, and a few inside jokes you’ll find if you look. The fish is serious. Sustainable, day-boat when we can get it, beer-battered when you ask nicely.
